STAY SAFE

Golden rules to never get scammed

Even before you reach for RakshakBot, these rules will protect you from 90% of scams.

01
Never share your OTP
No bank, telecom company, or government agency will ever ask for your OTP over a call or message. Ever. Period.
02
Verify the sender
Legitimate banks send SMS from short codes like HDFCBK or SBIATM — not from 10-digit mobile numbers.
03
Check URLs carefully
Look for subtle misspellings — sbi-login.net, hdfc-secure.co.in. Real bank sites end in .com/.in and have https://.
04
Urgency is a red flag
"Your account will be blocked in 2 hours" is a pressure tactic. Legitimate organizations give you time and proper channels.
05
Never pay to receive a prize
If someone asks for "processing fees" or "taxes" to release your winnings — it's always a scam. Always.
06
When in doubt, call the bank
If you receive a suspicious message claiming to be your bank, hang up and call the official number on the back of your card.
